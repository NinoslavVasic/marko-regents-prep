Why no SSAT questions on this site?

The SSAT is a private test owned by the Enrollment Management Association (EMA). Unlike the NY Regents — which the State Education Department publishes for free — official SSAT questions are copyrighted and require a paid subscription or guidebook.

Rather than show you AI-generated questions that might be wrong or different in style from the real test, here are the official sources we recommend instead:

Official prep (use these for SSAT):

  • SSAT Official Online Practice — 4 full-length tests + 1,000+ questions ($80/yr from EMA)
  • Official SSAT Guide Books (Upper Level) — printed practice tests from EMA
  • Free Mini Practice Test — diagnostic from the official site

Free supplementary resources:

  • Khan Academy — vocabulary, math, reading skills (free)
  • Hotchkiss school's library — ask your teacher for the Bearcats' SSAT prep recommendations
